    Panora

    Panora helps to capture pictures for further panorama processing.

    Panora shows the last image (with configurable opacity) on the left part of the screen - this serves as a guide for easily covering the whole 360 degrees with overlay pictures. Pictures are named after current project and numbered. Once done, just process the pictures with you favurite panorama processing software, such as Hugin, and enjoy your new panorama. :)

    NOTE: the images are saved to the Pictures directory
